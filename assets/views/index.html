Vessel

Cannabis dispensary Portland, Or

Vessel is a new OMMP Dispensary serving medical and recreational customers in NW Portland. Our mission is to provide high quality medicine at great prices in a very unique shop. We carry outdoor and indoor flower, with several organic choices. We also carry a number of edibles, vaporizers, glass waterpipes, and CBD products. Our knowledgeable patient care representatives will help you find the best medicine for you, guaranteed. At Vessel you can take your time to find the perfect medicine, you don't have to rush!

Highlights

  • All grass coming from our farm is Clean Green Certified, meaning no chemicals! Ever!

  • Our budtenders make it their top priority to help you find the perfect product for your needs.

  • We may be a tiny boutique, but we carry the best quality products at a affordable price.

FAQ

  • How old do I have to be to purchase cannabis products?

    You must 21 or older to purchase cannabis products recreationally in the state of Oregon.

  • What form of identification do I need?

    To enter our shop you must provide a state issued Driver's license, Passport, or birth certificate.
