About us

Vessel is a new OMMP Dispensary serving medical and recreational customers in NW Portland. Our mission is to provide high quality medicine at great prices in a very unique shop. We carry outdoor and indoor flower, with several organic choices. We also carry a number of edibles, vaporizers, glass waterpipes, and CBD products. Our knowledgeable patient care representatives will help you find the best medicine for you, guaranteed. At Vessel you can take your time to find the perfect medicine, you don't have to rush!
